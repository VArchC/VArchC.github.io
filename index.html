VArchC, the ArchC Approximate Computing framework

VArchC is an ArchC-based framework for validation and verification of hardware Approximate Computing techniques. VArchC is powered by ADeLe, a high-level descriptive language that represents hardware approximations.

VArchC, ADeLe and ArchC are tools designed in the Computer Systems Laboratory, Institute of Computing, University of Campinas.
